**What would you like to be added**:
This is a request to add 2 metrics:
* Age of pending workloads, by clusterqueue. Gauge metric, presentable as a time series.
* Time from decision to preempt a workload till the time the preemption completes (latency of preemption), by clusterqueue. 

**Why is this needed**:
We are facing issues in production where some jobs remain pending for a long time. So, we want the average/max times that jobs are waiting in each clusterqueue. The `kueue_admission_wait_time_seconds` gives a somewhat similar metric but only for admitted workloads, not pending ones. Also, it is a histogram, not a time series. To make things simpler, this could be the time till job admission from either job creation or eviction. 

Secondly, another issue we have seen is that, once Kueue decides to preempt a workload, it may take a while for the workload to terminate. That's because it may have finalizers, long pod grace period, volume unmount issues, etc.  It would be good to measure the 'preemption latency', i.e., the time from the decision to preempt to the time preemption completes (i.e., the other workload is gone). 


**Completion requirements**:
Have the metrics visible in Prometheus.
