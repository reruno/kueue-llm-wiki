### Comment by [@mszadkow](https://github.com/mszadkow) — 2025-05-23T14:12:01Z

I have done the research and there is only one way to be able to see the panic in parallel.
That is to use `--output-interceptor-mode=none`, as those are the panics not generated by the assertions and they land in the stdout.
I will compare CI runs, with and without this option to compare what exactly lands in the build_logs.txt and if something is missing if the panic occurs.
