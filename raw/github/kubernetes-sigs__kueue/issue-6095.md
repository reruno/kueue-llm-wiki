Thank you @windsonsea for your reaction. It helps to create a community where we are respectful of each other's time. 

Once you merge a couple of PRs:
- you can apply for membership which will relieve reviewers of giving /ok-to-test,
- you will learn more about best practices of preparing documentation PRs, eg. not to translate `Job`,
- you will agree with other reviewers on the consistent translation of some words which can be translated in different ways. 

These will allow you to open PRs in the future which require less review time.

Thank you for your contributions and we are looking for more.
