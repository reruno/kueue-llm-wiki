#### What this PR does / why we need it:
Currently, the Workload `Evicted` condition with an `InactiveWorkload` reason is added regardless of whether the Worklads already have an `Evicted` reason with other reasons like `Preempted` and `PodsReadyTimeout`.

But, we should keep the previous reason to avoid losing the truth reason of the eviction reason and double counting the eviction reason.
