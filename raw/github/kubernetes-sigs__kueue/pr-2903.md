#### What this PR does / why we need it:
We need to perform a deep clone of `ResourceGroups` instead of a shallow copy when taking a snapshot of `clusterQueue` to prevent race conditions.

> @mbobrovskyi Do you know the reason why we can not detect this race condition error in every CI Job?
IIUC, we perform the integration and unit tests with `-race` option, right?

The issue doesn't occur every time because, in most cases, we update LabelKeys before they are read by the scheduler goroutine. However, the race condition happens during the ResourceFlavor update (specifically, after setting the finalizer on the resource flavor [here](https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/kueue/blob/main/pkg/controller/core/resourceflavor_controller.go#L179-L181)).
