  > Any particular reason to introduce the Pod reconciler rather than LWS reconciler?
  
  The LWS reconciler approach will not work well because the STS has an OwnerReference to the LWS. When the LWS is removed, it will be deleted before the STS and Pods, which will in turn trigger the deletion of other dependent objects. If we delete the LWS before the STS and Pods, we will lose context, and the Pods will remain stuck with a finalizer.
  
  We can also remove finalizers from all Pods when the LWS is deleted, but this approach risks encountering flakes due to a race condition if Pods are being created while the LWS is being deleted.
