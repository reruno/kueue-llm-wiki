### Review by [@mbobrovskyi](https://github.com/mbobrovskyi) (commented) — 2025-02-04T12:52:49Z

`Pods` have an `ownerReference` and should be deleted after the `StatefulSet` is removed. I think the issue is that the `StatefulSet` is deleted, but the `Pods` still have a finalizer, preventing their deletion.

https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/kueue/blob/d02a7649e63fba77d546adaa8be11c72624009f5/test/util/util.go#L117-L119

And on this case we can't finalize `Pods` due to we lost context when deleted the `StatefulSet`.

https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/kueue/blob/d02a7649e63fba77d546adaa8be11c72624009f5/pkg/controller/jobs/statefulset/statefulset_reconciler.go#L62

If the `Pods` still exist, then the `Workload` also exists, preventing the `ClusterQueue` and the `ResourceFlavor` deletion. 

To fix it I think we should create additional `PodReconciler` like we have on `LeaderWorkerSet` [here](https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/kueue/blob/d02a7649e63fba77d546adaa8be11c72624009f5/pkg/controller/jobs/leaderworkerset/leaderworkerset_pod_reconciler.go). I already created prototype for it [here](https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/kueue/blob/4b13a29e5bc0eafbe064ae6dd2a9ee68e568ee10/pkg/controller/jobs/statefulset/statefulset_pod_reconciler.go) on the https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/kueue/pull/3951.

Alternatively, we can add a finalizer to `StatefulSets` and only finalize them after the `Pods` have been finalized.
