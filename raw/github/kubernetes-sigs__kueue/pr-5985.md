  Note for future me: You'll notice that this integration test does not include a check on whether the workload reservation was removed. 
  
  See: https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/kueue/issues/5937#issuecomment-3077597464
  
  > Even though the quota for cohort is reduced, the Workload still has the QuotaReserved condition. Kueue does not have a mechanism to preempt due to reduced quota (either by lowering the numbers or deleting a CQ contributing to the cohort's quota). So, as a result CQs and cohorts may run over-committed for a while.
