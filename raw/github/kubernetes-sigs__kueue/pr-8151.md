### Comment by [@mbobrovskyi](https://github.com/mbobrovskyi) — 2025-12-16T06:37:38Z

Here is the current logic:

1. Preempt the workload on the manager.
2. Copy eviction statuses (currently only Evict and Preemption) to the worker.
3. Complete the eviction on the worker and sync Job statuses.
4. Wait for the eviction to complete on the manager.
