### Review by [@PBundyra](https://github.com/PBundyra) (commented) — 2025-12-31T13:33:37Z

- **pkg/cache/queue/cluster_queue.go:1** by [@PBundyra](https://github.com/PBundyra) — 2025-12-31T13:33:37Z
  You're right, I shouldn't reveal the internal logic in V(2). However I'd like to provide users with some notion of what happens with a Workload if it's not admitted. We have logs in scheduler on V(2) which share that Kueue processes a Workload (`Attempting to schedule workload`), and if it succeeds there's also a log that confirms that (`Workload successfully admitted and assigned flavors`). When it fails Kueue only logs (`Workload re-queued`) but it's very vague. E.g. it can mean that there's currently no capacity and a Workloads needs to wait, and BestEffortFIFO will try a consecutive Workload, but it can also mean that Workload issued a preemption which is still ongoing. I'd like to differentiate those situations and log that at the time there was not enough capacity and Workload will be skipped in the next scheduling cycle. 
  
  Having said that, I'll correct those logs and provide more high-level information without revealing logic underneath
