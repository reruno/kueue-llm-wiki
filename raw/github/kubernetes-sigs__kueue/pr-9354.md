### Comment by [@mimowo](https://github.com/mimowo) — 2026-02-19T09:43:46Z

> IIUC, when TASProfileLeastFreeCapacity or TASProfileMixed are enabled, users can not use balanced placement in the following:

Yeah, the interaction between BalancedPlacement and Mixed is more subtle. I think there is still some interaction with Balanced and other profiles at two levels:
1. Balanced may not always be used, even if the feature gate enabled (as pointed out in the comment above)
2. BalancedPlacement could still balance Pods high level (on the preferred level and above), while still use BestFit and other approaches when stepping down the lower levels.
