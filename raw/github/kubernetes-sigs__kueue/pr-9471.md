#### What this PR does / why we need it:
Fix a test flaking in https://github.com/kubernetes-sigs/kueue/pull/9232#issuecomment-3957576106.

We are scheduling so fast after the workload finishes, that stale AFS values were being used in the scheduling cycle. After #9232, due to the small delay, there was a chance that one of the values was stale and one was up to date, as the LocalQueue reconciler ran in the meantime.

I take an approach proposed by @mimowo, simplifying the test by adding a hold policy to the CQ.
