To fix flake discovered why working on #9716. 

#### Special notes for your reviewer:

After reduction of the backoff I got flake more common at the assert `util.ExpectPendingWorkloadsMetric(cq1, 1, 1)`. However, this assert is not correct after the introduction of the asynchrnous requeueing of the workloads, because inadmissible workloads are put on the heap after 1s. The first workload, once it is put on the heap, it stays there behind the new sticky workload, and is not re-evaluated. The stable state is `util.ExpectPendingWorkloadsMetric(cq1, 2, 0)`.

So, I propose to capture the test intention by demonstrating the new  (second) workload gets admitted (after the preemptions complete), despite the first one was sticky originally.

I was running the test looped locally after the adjustment and 100 retries passed.
